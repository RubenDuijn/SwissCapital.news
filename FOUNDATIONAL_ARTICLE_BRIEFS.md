# Foundational Article Briefs

These are anchor pieces that define SwissCapital.news intellectual identity.

## 1) AI Is Concentrating Capital Faster Than Markets Realize
Purpose:
- flagship thesis at the intersection of AI infrastructure, concentration dynamics, and geopolitical leverage

Must cover:
- compute as strategic infrastructure
- concentration mechanisms (power, chips, cloud distribution)
- incentive alignment across states, hyperscalers, and enterprises
- capital allocation consequences

## 2) The Quiet Return of Industrial Policy
Purpose:
- establish global policy and strategic-industry credibility

Must cover:
- states as capital allocators
- semiconductors, energy, logistics, reshoring
- implementation capacity vs policy rhetoric
- investment implications across jurisdictions

## 3) Why Trust Is Becoming a Financial Premium
Purpose:
- define SwissCapital institutional worldview

Must cover:
- trust as economic infrastructure
- governance credibility and funding costs
- fragmentation and transaction friction
- procedural legitimacy as valuation input

## 4) Political Capital Flows and the Incentive Economy
Purpose:
- introduce political economy lens without sensationalism

Must cover:
- policy incentives vs public rhetoric
- procurement, tax architecture, subsidy design
- how capital quietly follows incentive structures
- framework for analyzing policy-execution quality

## 5) The New Geography of Strategic Capital
Purpose:
- provide systems map across AI, energy, defense, semiconductors, and logistics

Must cover:
- regionalization of strategic capital
- energy and infrastructure constraints
- alliance structure and legal reliability as investment filters
- long-cycle implications for institutions

## Publishing Rule
Publish slowly and selectively.

If a piece does not add structural insight, do not publish it.
