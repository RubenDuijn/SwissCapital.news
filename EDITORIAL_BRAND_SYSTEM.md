# SwissCapital.news Editorial Brand System

## Core Positioning
SwissCapital.news - Intelligence on capital, technology, and global systems.

## Identity
SwissCapital.news is a premium global intelligence publication focused on capital, technology, geopolitical systems, infrastructure, incentives, and structural change.

The publication should feel:
- institutional
- calm
- analytical
- restrained
- intelligent
- established
- globally aware
- Swiss-inspired in precision and tone

It must never feel:
- like a startup
- like a personal blog
- like a creator newsletter
- like crypto media
- like AI spam
- like clickbait finance content
- like conspiracy media
- like sensationalist journalism

## Editorial Mission
SwissCapital.news examines how capital, institutions, technology, and geopolitical systems interact beneath the surface of current events.

## Credibility Rules
- Do not invent analyst personas or unverified editorial biographies.
- Use institutional bylines such as SwissCapital Research, SwissCapital Analysis, or Editorial Desk.
- Anchor strategic essays in named companies, policy frameworks, official disclosures, and public-source references.
- The site should signal established publication standards through archive depth, sourcing, dates, and methodology clarity.

## Tone and Writing Standard
Tone:
- measured
- high-signal
- systems-oriented
- editorial
- strategic

Writing style:
- sophisticated but readable
- analytical rather than emotional
- focused on structural implications
- no hype language
- no exaggerated certainty
- no startup energy
- no motivational framing
- no outrage framing

## Article Quality Bar
Every article should:
- identify structural shifts
- connect multiple domains
- analyze incentives
- explain second-order effects
- surface signal from noise

Avoid:
- daily news churn
- shallow SEO content
- generic summaries
- mass-produced AI tone
- personality-driven commentary
- unsupported macro claims without named evidence
- elegant but thin executive-summary copy

## Strategic Clarification
SwissCapital.news is not:
- a newsletter brand
- a creator economy product
- a trading signals platform
- a conspiracy outlet

The newsletter is secondary distribution infrastructure, not identity.

## Visual Direction
- Swiss editorial minimalism
- restrained typography
- monochrome or muted palette
- elegant whitespace
- premium magazine feel
- institutional calm

Reference direction:
- Financial Times
- The Economist
- Bloomberg
- Monocle
- modern Swiss editorial design

## Standard Article Structure
1. Opening thesis
2. Structural analysis
3. Incentive analysis
4. Capital implications
5. Long-term systemic interpretation

## Launch Doctrine
Do not publish weak filler content.

It is better to publish:
- 1 exceptional article
than:
- 10 generic AI-generated pieces

The first wave of articles must define the publication worldview and establish immediate editorial seriousness.

## Current Editorial Risk and Fix
Risk:
- conceptually strong but editorially thin

Wrong solution:
- more articles
- faster posting
- SEO filler

Right solution:
- a small number of disproportionately strong foundational pieces
- sparse and confident homepage
- selective distribution

Target reader reaction:
- This publication already has an editorial worldview.
Not:
- Someone just started experimenting with AI articles.
